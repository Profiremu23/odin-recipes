Apple Pie Recipe

The apple pie is a simple, but still a tasty and unbeatable dessert for everyone. If we keep on digging deeper into old recipes, we can still find something new. There are people still making apple pies with family recipes, with the dough being boosted with extra ingredients, while others decide on twilling the filling, but one thing is sure, whatever recipe is going to the oven, the result always puts a smile on you with your family or friends.

Apple Pie Ingredients

For the dough

  • 1 packet of baking powder
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 500 grams of flour
  • 150 grams of butter
  • 2 pieces of egg
  • 6 tablespoons of sugar
  • 4 tablespoons of sour cream
  • Some bread crumbs

For the filling

  • 1 kg of red apple
  • 3 tablespoons of brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of ground cinnamon
  • Lime juice

For the top

  • Icing sugar

Steps To Make An Apple Pie

  1. Mix the baking powder, salt and flour for the dough, then crumble it with the cold butter. Add the eggs, sugar, and mix thoroughly. Refrigerate for at least half an hour.
  2. Peel the apples, grate them on a large-hole grater and squeeze them. Season with sugar, cinnamon and lemon juice.
  3. After cooling, divide the dough into 2 part and roll it out on a floured surface for approx. to a thickness of half a centimeter.
  4. Line the bottom of a 38x26 cm baking tray with baking paper, place the first portion of rolled out dough on it, prick it with a fork and sprinkle it thinly with breadcrumbs. Spread the apple filling on it, then cover it with the other half of the rolled out dough. We also prick the top.
  5. Bake for approximately 30-40 minutes in an oven preheated to 180Â°C with air stirring on. When cooled, sprinkle with powdered sugar and cut into cubes for serving.
