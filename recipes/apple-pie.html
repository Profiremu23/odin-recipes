Apple Pie Recipe

The apple pie is an simple, but still a tasteful and unbeatable dessert for everyone. If we keep on digging deeper on old recipes, we can still find something new. There are people still making apple pies with family recipes, with the pasta being boosted with extra ingredient, while others decide on twilling the filling, but one thing is sure, whatever recipe is going to the oven, the result always put a smile on the face.
