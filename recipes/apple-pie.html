Apple Pie Recipe

The apple pie is a simple, but still a tasty and unbeatable dessert for everyone. If we keep on digging deeper into old recipes, we can still find something new. There are people still making apple pies with family recipes, with the dough being boosted with extra ingredients, while others decide on twilling the filling, but one thing is sure, whatever recipe is going to the oven, the result always puts a smile on you with your family or friends.

Apple Pie Ingredients

  • For the dough: one packet of baking powder, one pinch of salt, 500 grams of flour, 150 grams of butter, two pieces of egg, six tablespoons of sugar, four tablespoons of sour cream, some bread crumbs

  • For the filling: one kilograms of red apple, three tablespoons of brown sugar, one tablespoon of ground cinnamon, lime juice

  • For the top: icing sugar

Steps To Make An Apple Pie

  1. For the dough, we mix the baking powder with the salt and the flour, then we crush with the cold butter together. Then we add the eggs, the sugar and the sour cream, and we mush them properly. Then we put to the fridge for half an hour.
